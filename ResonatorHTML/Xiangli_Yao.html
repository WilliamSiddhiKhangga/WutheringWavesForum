Xiangli Yao Overview

In Wuthering Waves, Xiangli Yao is a 5-Stars Electro character and uses Gauntlet during the fight.

In a normal state, Xiangli Yao's Forte Circuit energy is called “Capacity,” with a maximum limit of 100 points, primarily obtained through Resonance Skill and Basic Attack. When “Capacity” reaches 100, Xiangli Yao's RS (Resonance Skill) will be enhanced, doubling its damage. When used, he leaps into the air, and if he uses Basic Attack mid-air, it triggers an enhanced Mid-Air Attack.

When Xiangli Yao uses Resonance Liberation, it deals massive damage and grants him 3 “Hypercubes.” The Forte Circuit then transforms into “Performance Capacity”, with a limit of 5 points. His RS is enhanced into another form, where each use of the enhanced RS in the Liberation state consumes one “Hypercube.” After consuming all 3 Hypercubes, he exits the Liberation state. When using the enhanced RS, he can also follow up with an enhanced Mid-Air Attack. “Performance Capacity” can be generated by using enhanced RS in the Liberation state (2 points), enhanced Mid-Air Attack (3 points), and the 3-stage enhanced Basic Attack in the Liberation state (a total of 5 points). When “Performance Capacity” reaches 5 points, the ultimate enhanced RS can be used.


Pros

    Basic Attack
    Perform up to 5 consecutive attacks, dealing Electro DMG.

    Mid-air Attack
    Use Basic Attack after a successful Dodge to attack the target, dealing Electro DMG.

    Heavy Attack
    Perform a charged attack at the cost of STA, dealing Electro DMG.

    Dodge Counter
    Use Basic Attack after a successful Dodge to attack the target, dealing Electro DMG.

    Deduction - Resonance Skill

    Attack the target, dealing Electro DMG.

    Cogitation - Resonance Liberation

    Attack the target, dealing Electro DMG. Enter Intuition. When in Intuition: -Obtain 3 Hypercube(s). Each time Resonance Skill Law of Reigns is cast, consume 1 Hypercube(s). Intuition ends once all Hypercubes are consumed. -Basic Attack and Heavy Attack are replaced with Basic Attack Pivot - Impale, which performs up to 3 consecutive attacks, dealing Electro DMG. -Resonance Skill Deduction is replaced with Resonance Skill Divergence, which deals Electro DMG. -Dodge Counter is replaced with Dodge Counter Unfathomed, considered as Resonance Liberation DMG.

    Forever Seeking - Forte Circuit

    Resonance SKill - Decipher
    When Capacity reaches 100, Resonance Skill Deduction is replaced with Resonance Skill Decipher. Consume 100 Capacity to cast Resonance Skill Decipher, dealing Electro DMG, considered as Resonance Liberation DMG.

    Resonance Skill - Law of Reigns
    When in Intuition triggered by Resonance Liberation and Performance Capacity reaches 5, Resonance Skill Divergence is replaced with Resonance Skill Law of Reigns. Consume 5 Performance Capacity to cast Resonance Skill Law of Reigns, dealing Electro DMG, considered as Resonance Liberation DMG.

    Mid-airAttack - Revamp
    Shortly after casting Resonance Skill Decipher or Resonance Skill Divergence, use Basic Attack to perform Mid-air Attack Revamp at the cost of STA, dealing Electro DMG, considered as Resonance Liberation DMG.

    Capacity
    Xiangli Yao can hold up to 100 Capacity. Every Normal Attack Probe on hit grants Capacity. Every Resonance Skill Deduction on hit grants Capacity.

    Mid-airAttack - Revamp
    Xiangli Yao can hold up to 5 Performance Capacity. When in Intuition triggered by Resonance Liberation: Obtain 1 Performance Capacity when Stage 1 of Basic Attack Pivot - Impale hits a target. Obtain 2 Performance Capacity when Stage 2 or 3 of Basic Attack Pivot - Impale hits a target. Obtain 2 Performance Capacity for every Resonance Skill Divergence on hit. Obtain 3 Performance Capacity for every Mid-air Attack Revamp on hit. Obtain 2 Performance Capacity for every Dodge Counter Unfathomed on hit.
